About Company

SS Chemical innovates, excels, and supports customers with superior, cost-effective products.

SS Chemical was incorporated by a group of engineers with the mission of developing & manufacturing a specialty in minerals through indigenous research & application-based technology for the next millennium. In interaction with premier institutions in India, the company has developed a technology of its own class to give the best shape, size, and narrow cuts. The filler produced imparts a higher filler loading, better dispersion, and effective dispersion in various applications.

Why Choose Us?

Our Success Journey Choose We can Provide.

  • Our product boasts sharp cuts, a lower average particle size, a large surface area, and a better surface finish. These characteristics contribute to improved performance and higher quality in various applications, setting our product apart from competitors.

  • Requiring less lubricant and carrying minimal water, our product ensures efficient processing and superior end-product quality. This low demand for lubricants and reduced water content lead to cost savings and enhanced efficiency in manufacturing processes.

  • Featuring smooth, rounded edges compared to other fillers with sharp, jagged edges, our product provides a superior finish. This unique attribute enhances the aesthetic and functional quality of the final products, making it an ideal choice for precision applications.

  • The evenly distributed and closely packed particles of our product enhance its flow properties significantly. This results in better handling and processing, ensuring consistent and reliable performance across a wide range of industrial and manufacturing applications.
